The first time you edit a dog, you are presented with a dialog that has the following four pages:

  • Properties
  • Titles
  • Registration Numbers
  • Existing Points

Properties

The only field that you absolutely must fill out is the Call Name. All other fields are optional. Though you probably will want to set the date of birth to the correct date!

The Notes field is for an miscellaneous data you may want to record, for instance, an embedded chip id or city license number.

Titles

This page is for recording an titles that you earn. After entering a title via a menu item or the toolbar , you will be taken to this page.

The "View Hidden" option (also available on the main "View->Show Hidden Titles" menu), allows you to hide titles you no longer want to see. There are two occasions you may want to hide a title:

  • The title has been superceded by a later title. For instance, earning your MX in AKC implies that you have earned your AX.
  • The title is one you will never earn. For instance, if you started competing in USDAA prior to 2004 and are currently in Masters, you will never earn the new game titles.
